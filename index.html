BTWZZMAX Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 20, 2026

Support: BTWZZMAX@icloud.com

Overview

BTWZZMAX is a personal holdings and activity journal for digital asset records. This policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Local Data Storage

All user data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

  • Holdings entries, journal activities, notes, and cost basis values remain on your device.
  • Reference photos you attach are saved in the app’s local storage area.
  • CSV exports are created on your device and shared only when you choose a share action.
  • No account registration is required to use BTWZZMAX.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Name, email address, phone number, or government identifiers
  • Device advertising identifiers for tracking purposes
  • Financial account credentials or wallet private keys
  • Location history or contacts

Device Permissions

BTWZZMAX may request access to your camera or photo library solely so you can attach optional reference images to holdings or journal entries. Images are stored locally and are not uploaded by the app for collection purposes.

Not Financial Advice

BTWZZMAX does not provide financial advice, trading services, live market prices, or investment recommendations. It is a record-keeping utility only.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your records remain on your device until you edit or delete them within the app, or until you remove the app from your device. Uninstalling BTWZZMAX removes locally stored app data from your device.

Children’s Privacy

BTWZZMAX is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy to reflect product changes. The effective date at the top of this page will be revised when updates occur.

Summary: BTWZZMAX is built for local personal record keeping. Your entries stay on your device under your control.
